Workers' Compensation

Prevention, Claims and OSHA are Just the Beginning

An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure. That’s why many businesses know the front-end investment in a customized Workers’ Compensation program is worth its weight in gold. At HRG, we manage (and monitor) this often overlooked, yet critical piece of your HR program – so you can focus on mining more gold.

The best time for a risk analysis is anytime before a risk has time to develop and we maintain regular analysis intervals. In addition, Our HR Outsourcing solution comes with other preventative programs to include safety meetings and ongoing training, safety audits and manual updates, workers’ compensation claims administration, OSHA compliance assistance and programs to foster a drug-free workplace - all critical components of an effective Workers’ Compensation management program.

We are very proud, and protective, of our clients’ collective Workers’ Compensation Experience Modifier. We work hard to maintain our Ex-mod because our clients rely on our diligence to keep their coverage costs in check. A quick evaluating of your rating and claims history will help us determine if inclusion in our program makes sense and can benefit your company.
