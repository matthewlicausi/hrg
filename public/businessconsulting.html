Our Services

HRG offers clients a fast path to results through our full suite of sales, partner and business development consulting services.

Although each HRG client engagement involves a customized statement of work, the services provided can be classified within the following categories:

STRATEGIC SALES PLAN DEVELOPMENT


Working closely with clients to structure effective go-to-market strategies and action plans.

CHANNEL AND DISTRIBUTION STRATEGY & ANALYSIS

Identifying, building and maintaining clients' ideal channels to market.

PERSONNEL EVALUATION, MODIFICATION & PLACEMENT

Ensuring clients employ top tier and the most relevant sales personnel required to achieve stated objectives.

SALES PROCESS & REPORTING DESIGN


Putting in place management reporting and analytic toolsets required to effectively monitor, measure and adapt.

KEY PROSPECT ENGAGEMENT


Serving as clients’ front-line sales resource for engaging key accounts and strategic channel partners.

PRODUCT MESSAGE & PRESENTATION DEVELOPMENT

Collaborating with client product marketing and sales leadership to craft compelling product collateral material.
