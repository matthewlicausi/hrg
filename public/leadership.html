Leadership

Beau Peters - President

Beau established HRG in 2002 after 20 years of experience in sales, sales management and channel development with a strong emphasis in telecommunications technology for the global carrier market place.

Prior to HRG, Peters worked for both large corporations and a variety of start-up companies where he set the bar for sales success. Throughout the course of his career, Peters has held a variety of US Domestic and International assignments while building a substantial worldwide contact base that he fully leverages on behalf of HRG clients.

Peters' primary focus for HRG clients is building successful new market launch initiatives for companies in the telecommunications and mobile digital media sectors. In this role, he serves as the primary customer facing resource, and coordinator of all other HRG personnel needed to fulfill clients' specific sales and business development objectives.
