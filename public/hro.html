Workers' Compensation

Prevention, Claims and OSHA are Just the Beginning

An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure. That’s why many businesses know the front-end investment in a customized Workers’ Compensation program is worth its weight in gold. At HRO, we manage (and monitor) this often overlooked, yet critical piece of your HR program – so you can mine more gold.

The best part about a risk analysis? When it happens before the risk occurs. Other preventative programs such as safety training, audits and reviews ensure you’ve done all you can to manage risk, and save money.

Since Workers’ Compensation issues don’t stop there, we don’t, either. Assistance with safety meetings, monitoring of safety manuals for compliance, managing claims administration, OSHA compliance assistance and fostering a drug-free workplace environment are all critical areas of a seamless Workers’ Compensation program, and why the’re essential service offerings to our clients.
