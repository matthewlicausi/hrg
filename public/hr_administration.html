Human Resource Services

Employee Benefits, Federal Compliance and everything in between

Harvest Resource Group offers a seasoned team of designated HR professionals, payroll and benefits specialists, accountants and years of experience in the HR Trenches. we allow employers to completely outsource all aspects of Human Resource administration, compliance and even the risk associated with HR entanglements. Employee on boarding, termination, job descriptions, handbooks, benefits sign up, modifications and claims management, payroll, workers' comp management and audits, 401K programs, Section 125 plans, tax filings, harassment issues, discrimination claims,… HRG handles it all. We can even provide health insurance and workers’ compensation coverage options.

We are certain this is not new news to you.
