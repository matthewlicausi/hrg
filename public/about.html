About Us



      

Harvest Resource Group

Established to provide sales and business development consulting services in 2002, HRG has provided guidance to a wide range of companies for over 12 years in the areas of sales, marketing and channel partner development. Building successful new market launch, or re-launch initiatives to attain specific sales and business development objectives is HRG’s primary focus for clients.

While some of HRG’s clients are based in the US, many are from overseas and often require assistance with HR regulatory compliance and establishing comprehensive benefit solutions for their employees. To satisfy this need, HRG offers comprehensive HR Outsourcing solutions so that clients are able to focus on building their business rather than the increasingly complex issues US-based companies must navigate to properly manage and maintain high quality, productive employees.

Harvest Resource Group, Inc. was founded by Beau Peters after 20 years in a variety of global sales and business development roles at both established and start-up companies. You can connect with Beau on Linked, or reach out to him directly at bpeters@harvestresourcegroup.com to explore how HRG can play a role in your company’s success.
