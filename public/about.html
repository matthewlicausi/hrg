Human Resources Outsourcing - HRO

A Kansas City based company, Harvest Resource Group's HRO division provides human resources (HR) services, payroll services, workers’ compensation and staffing services.

The HRO portfolio of services is made up of HRO Outsourcing – a human resources outsourcing (HRO) provider, HRO Payroll Solutions – a full-service payroll services provider, and HRO Staffing – a full-service recruiting and staffing services provider.
